Position Properties

Understanding the different position properties in CSS.

Types of Positions

  • Static: The default position. Elements are positioned according to the normal flow of the document.
  • Relative: Positioned relative to its normal position.
  • Absolute: Positioned relative to the nearest positioned ancestor (not static).
  • Fixed: Positioned relative to the viewport, meaning it always stays in the same place even when scrolled.
  • Sticky: A hybrid of relative and fixed. It toggles between relative and fixed, depending on the scroll position.
